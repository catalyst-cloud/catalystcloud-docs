Catalyst Cloud Metrics Service
******************************

Resources are now automatically marked as deleted after 30 days
===============================================================

:ref:`Resources <metrics-resources>` in the Catalyst Cloud Metrics Service
that have not had any measures published to them for at least 30 days now
automatically have their "ended at" timestamp set, to reflect the fact they
have either been deleted or been inactive for that amount of time.

This helps to improve the performance of queries for active resources by
reducing the amount of resources being counted as "active" to only ones
that have recently had measures published. This is relevant in the rare case
that delete notifications are missed (causing the resource in the Metrics Service
to not be updated accordingly), or for resource types that are not automatically
marked as deleted (for more information, see the :ref:`FAQ <metrics-faq-deleted-not-ended>`).
